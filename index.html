Biography

I am now working as a Research Assistant in Tsinghua University, Beijing.

My research focuses on computer vision and deep learning, with emphasis on providing better algorithms for machine to understand the world. My current work attempts to extract information of objects presented in RGB and RGB-D images which can be useful for real world applications.

I received my M.S. degree in Control Science and Technology from Tsinghua University in 2017 advised by Prof. Xiangyang Ji, where I worked with Dr. Jifeng Dai at Microsoft Research Asia. I received bachelor degree in Automation from Tsinghua University in 2014.

I'm applying for phd enrolled in Fall 2018
