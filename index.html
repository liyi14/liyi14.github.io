Biography

I am now a visiting student in Computer Science & Engineering Department at the University of Washington, Seattle, advised by Prof. Dieter Fox, after receiving my M.S. degree in 2014 major in Control Science and Technology in 2014 advised by Prof. Xiangyang Ji and bachelor degree major in Automation in 2010 from Tsinghua University. My research focuses on computer vision and deep learning, with emphasis on providing better solutions for machine to understand the world. My current work attempts to extract information of objects presented in RGB and RGB-D images which can be useful for real world applications.

I'm applying for phd enrolled in Fall 2018
